ClipSqueeze Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how ClipSqueeze handles user information. ClipSqueeze is a modern video compressor application that helps users reduce video size quickly while maintaining high quality.

Features

  • Fast video compression
  • Smart quality presets
  • Save storage space
  • Share compressed videos instantly
  • Dark mode support

Information Collection and Use

ClipSqueeze does not collect, store, or share personal information. All video processing happens locally on the user's device.

The application does not upload videos, photos, or personal files to external servers.

Permissions

The app may request storage or media access permissions only for selecting and compressing videos from your device.

Third-Party Services

ClipSqueeze does not use third-party services that collect personal data.

Data Security

Since all processing is done locally on your device, your files remain private and secure.

Children's Privacy

ClipSqueeze does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
