# ClipSqueeze Privacy Policy This repository contains the official privacy policy page for the ClipSqueeze mobile application. ClipSqueeze is a modern video compressor app that helps users reduce video size quickly while maintaining quality. ## Features - Fast video compression - Smart quality presets - Save storage space - Share compressed videos instantly - Dark mode support ## Privacy The app processes videos locally on the user's device and does not upload personal files to external servers. ## Official App ClipSqueeze – Video Compressor & Size Reducer
